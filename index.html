Information

Need information quick? Don't worry we gotcha!

ADP

You wanna get ya check right? APD is an application used to set up your direct deposit and where timesheets are done!

Pingboard

Information Overload. Pingboard is the application that holds info of all employees. Names, Pictures, Bdays, Positions, etc!

Homepool

You think it, You search. Homepool has all the information about the company on it. You can use the search bar to find almost anything!

Concur

Uber everywhere. Concur is an application that handles all your travel expenses for company trips!

iOffice

Lost? iOffice is a tool that shows the layout of every Datto office space. So finding the kitchens, game rooms, and bathrooms are easy. You can also search room names or employees names to find their desks!

Slack

Just slack me. Slack is a tool used to communicate with everyone working at Datto. They have channels/group chats for each individual office, leftover food, new hires, inclusion initiatives, etc!
